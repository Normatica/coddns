Hi!

Need access to your private server at home from Internet? Or do you want to see the surveillance cameras in your home? You may prefer to access your personal media from anywhere without having to carry a removable hard drive or hire (and paid...) a static IP, or have to confirm your email every month to keep the service ...

That's why we're here!

With CODDNS you will always have a label through which you'll be able to access your home network without having to worrying about changes in the public IP address given for router from your ISP. Simply associates a label available your public IP address, install the IP updater and access your computer from anywhere on the Internet.

You can see the first original and awesome CODDNS system working fine on http://coddns.org

Feel free to use.
